Philosophy

Quiet objects for people who notice details.

SimpleMinima is an independent studio exploring physical objects, typography, process, and atmosphere through minimalist culture and human-centered imperfection.

We are interested in quiet visuals, functional systems, thoughtful materials, and the beauty of things made slowly.

Our work lives somewhere between design studio, workshop, archive, and visual diary.

Process

The process is part of the object.

Every piece begins as observation.

A terminal command.

A forgotten phrase.

A paper texture.

A workflow sketch.

A late night experiment.

A photograph taken quietly.

We prototype slowly, print in small batches, document honestly, and refine through use.
